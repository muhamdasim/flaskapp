FAQ

1Can I start on one plan and upgrade to another?

Yes, of course, just click on the get started button on the new plan and we will take care of the rest.

2What is the difference between price plans?

Each plan offers a different number of features and is based upon your own activity. Light users can start with the Learner Plan, whereas Heavy Users can start on the Pro User Plan

3Will my plan renew automatically?

Yes, your plan renews automatically.

4Do unused searches etc. roll over to the next month?

No – each month resets for features.

5Can I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel the renewal of your plan at any time for any reason. Click on my account, go to the account tab, and click ‘cancel’.
