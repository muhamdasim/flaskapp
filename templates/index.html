Search Tips

If we were to put on a webinar or course on how to search on Google, most people would say, are you kidding, I know how to search on Google. We believe that most people may not be using Google search to its full potential, so here are some tips that we hope you find useful:

Search for an exact match.
For example

"tallest building"

Exclude words from search.
For example:

Mustang -cars

Combine searches.
For example:

chocolate OR white chocolate

Keeping it simple and narrow your search.
For example

Pizza near me
Pizza places nearby

Location based search.
For example

pizza location: Calgary
loc:"Calgary" pizza
